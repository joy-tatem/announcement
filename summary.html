about

Fist of The North Star is a manga written and illustrated by Buronson and Tetsuo Hara, respectively. Toei Animation adopted the entire manga as an anime series (rare honor in this industry).

The series takes place in a post-apocalyptic alternate future. After a nuclear holocaust decimates all but 30% of earth's population, the remaining humans fight for scraps. It's a harsh world: food and water may be rare but kindness is nearly extinct.

Our story follows Kenshiro: a young man who inherited the undefeatable North Star technique.

Ken is not a man of many words but he only needs four: you are already dead. No criminal or bully is safe around Ken's Northern Star technique. Kenshiro taps into pressure points, killing his opponents with tension from their own muscles. He becomes a hero of the people.

Kenshiro's apperance seems to be modelded after Bruce Lee
